Mable logo is out

2017-01-10

Mable Logo

We have made and released the logo for Mable!

Our logo is composed of the name “Mable” and the backboard image. We arranged street lines and a grain pattern as a feature on a backboard. It has been decided that our brand color is #54d08e only. Because it should use a single color to print it on Mable using a laser cuttor.

Mable Instagram

There is one more thing that we would like to inform you of.

We started to post pictures/videos on Instagram (@mable.project) for sharing our concept and images. It will be about three themes of our project: Fab, OpenStreetMap, Projection Mapping.

We use Facebook and GitHub as another social media. Our Facebook page is created for communicating Japanese community, and several tools for Mable will be published on GitHub. So you can talk openly and freely with us.

Follow me if you interested our project!
